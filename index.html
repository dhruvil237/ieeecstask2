Gojo Satoru

Satoru Gojo is one of the main protagonists of Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at the Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College.


Itadori Yuji

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. After becoming Sukuna's vessel, Yuji began attending Tokyo Jujutsu High.


Kugisaki Nobara

Nobara Kugisaki is the tritagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. She is a first-year at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College.
